Social Media Checklist for College Students 
The Do's:-
1. Do Curate a Professional Image: Use platforms like LinkedIn to highlight your academic achievements, projects at VIT Bhopal, and technical skills.
2. Do Use Privacy Settings: Regularly review your account security settings to control who can see your personal posts and tagged photos.
3. Do Think Before You Post: Ask yourself if your content could negatively impact your future career or university reputation before hitting "share".
4. Do Network Meaningfully: Follow industry leaders, join academic groups, and engage in constructive discussions relevant to Computer Science.
5. Do Fact-Check Information: Verify the source of news or academic resources before sharing them to prevent the spread of digital misinformation.

The Don'ts:-
1. Don’t Post Offensive Content: Avoid sharing discriminatory, hateful, or unprofessional language that violates student codes of conduct.
2. Don’t Overshare Personal Data: Never post your home address, phone number, or sensitive university ID details publicly.
3. Don’t Engage with Trolls: Avoid heated online arguments; maintain a professional tone even when disagreeing with others.
4. Don’t Ignore Copyright: Avoid using images, music, or code in your posts without proper attribution or permission.
5. Don’t Post While Emotional: Refrain from posting vents or "rants" about professors, classmates, or companies, as these can be permanently archived.
